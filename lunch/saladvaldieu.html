Salade Val Dieu

Warm cheese, nuts, apple, bacon. 21.50



Remarks

This is a lunch salad so make sure the ammount is good enough.
Tomato, cucumber, onion, wallnuts, 3 slices of bacon, melt half val-dieu cheese with honey in the oven, garnish with green salad pea. Serve on beige plate with bread and butter on the side



Allergenics


GLUTEN: wheat, rye and barley

MILK: cheese

MOSTARD: salad dressing

SOJA and SESAM: wallnuts

NUTS: wallnuts

SULFUR DIOXIDE: apples (cooked with lemon juice)
