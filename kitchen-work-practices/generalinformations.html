How to use the checklists system:

Fill out the date and the responsable person for each task. All fields are required. Click in save as PDF and you will have a copy of the filled checklist in PDF format that can be sent directly to the kitchen group.



Kitchen dressing code:

All employees are required to dress properly with kitchen uniform. This includes safety shoes and hairnet.



Smoking/drinking/drug use/medication:

Limited smoking is allowed during your shift, provided there is time for this and your colleagues are aware of your absence. Smoking is only allowed at the hotel door, so not on the terrace or upstairs in the staff room! Drinking and drug use during or before your shift is not allowed. You must report any medication that may affect you before the start of your shift. This is for your safety and that of your colleague
