Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs & rustic bread - add salmon +4. 11.50



Remarks

Pour approximately 200ml of the liquid egg and scramble it untill done, bake the salmon together with the eggs if extra salmon is ordered. Add a touch of butter and take out of the heat just before the egg is fully cooked as the residual heat can make the eggs too dry.
Serve with bread, salad and butter. Garnish with fresh greens



Allergenics


EGG: scrambled egg

GLUTEN: wheat

MILK: butter

MOSTARD: salad dressing
