Remarks

1kg mussels each portion, add vegetables salt and peper.
Natural: nothing else needed.
Garlic: cooking cream and 1 spoon of garlic puree
White wine: aproximately 150ml of white wine
Provencal: provencal mix(or just oregano), lemon juice and sriracha
Serve on black plate with napkin, mayo, aioli, wet towel and fries on the side



Allergenics


EGG and MOSTARD: mayo

GLUTEN: wheat (fries)

MOLLUSCS: mussels

SELERY: vegetables

MILK: cooking cream (garlic only)
