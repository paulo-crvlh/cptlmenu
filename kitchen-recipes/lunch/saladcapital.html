Remarks

This is a lunch salad so make sure the ammount is good enough.
Tomato, cumcumber, onion, 2 chicken pieces (cut in half), 3 slices of bacon and 3 scampi. Garnish with cocktail sauce and green salad peas.
Serve on the BEIGE plate with bread and butter on the side.
