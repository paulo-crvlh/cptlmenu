Ceasar Salad King Prawns

Roman Salad, Parmesan cheese, croutons & Caesar dressing. small/large 19.5 | 23.5



Remarks

Use the deep plate for the salads. dont fill it up too much if its a small one, the large must be fuller.
add roman salad, little gem lettuce, croutons and parmesan, put extra dressing on top making lines. finish with the prawns and scampis, red beet for the color, bread and butter on the side.
Large: 4 prawns and 3 scampis
Small: 3 Prawns and 2 scampis



Baking the Prawns:

No need to peel then off, let then dry and bake with oil and butter (50/50) 2 minutes each side. During cooking, pour some of the fats on the prawns heads so it doesnt burn and become black.



Allergenics


GLUTEN: wheat

MILK: cheese, butter (shrimps) and caesar dressing

FISH: anchoves (dressing)

SHELLFISH: shrimps and prawns
