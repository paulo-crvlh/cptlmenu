Greek Salad

Sweet onions, tomato, cucumber, olives & feta cheese. 17,50



Remarks

In a bowl, mix a handful of tomatoes, cucumber, roman salad, then add a bit of olives, white onions, oregano, olive oil and balsamic vinager.
Mix well and serve on a deep plate. Garnish with feta blocks and give bread and butter on the side.
There is no small or large size for this salad, so follow the standard ammount.



Allergenics


GLUTEN: wheat

MILK: feta and butter
