Poké Bowl CAPITAL

Salmon or chicken, avocado, edamame & sesame rice. 18.50



Remarks

Serve on the deep pasta plate. Put the rice in the middle and the toppings arround: edamame, avocado in cubes, orange melon in cubes, cucumber, carrots with dressing and 2 pieces of little gem lettuce.
the protein is salmon (cold) or chicken (warm) and it goes on top of the poppadum. Cover the rice with miso sauce, a flower and sesame seeds. Ponzu sauce on the side.
